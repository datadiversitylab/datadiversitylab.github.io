Our conference room

Our conference room is designed to accommodate 7-8 individuals and is equipped with state-of-the-art technology for seamless meetings and gatherings. Lab cluster

Unparalleled computing performance

Our lab is equipped with a cutting-edge dell precision 7920 server, featuring dual intel xeon gold 5217 CPUs, an NVIDIA RTX A5000 (24GB GDDR6) graphics card, 192GB of DDR4 RAM, and a lightning-fast 4TB M.2 NVMe SSD. This powerful setup enables us to handle complex data processing tasks with ease.

UA HPC cluster

UA HPC cluster

We also have access to the University of Arizona's high performance computing (HPC) cluster. This system provides exceptional capabilities for high throughput computing, distributed memory parallel computing, and shared memory parallel computing. For detailed specifications and access information, please refer to the UA HPC website: https://rc.arizona.edu/hpc-htc/computingsystems

Data management

Data management

To securely store our research data for the long term, we utilize a synology DS723+ network attached storage (NAS) device. This provides a centralized and reliable data management solution.
