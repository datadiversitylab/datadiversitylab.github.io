Preparing for an advising meeting

Please review the following information below. This will make it so our conversation can be as fruitful and efficient as possible!

- Handbooks

If you are seeking for academic advise on the MS in Data Science and MS in Information Science at the iSchool (UArizona), please review the the relevant student handbooks before scheduling a meeting.

Data Science: https://ischool.arizona.edu/ms-data-science

Information Science: https://ischool.arizona.edu/ms-information-science/student-resources

- Advising appointment decorum

Please include an informative statement on the topic(s) that you would like to discuss during our meeting.

We will both will receive an email confirming your appointment with me. Keep that e-mail in your records.

If you were able to address your question/concern before the meeting, please cancel your appointment in advance.

Please schedule a new meeting if you know that you will be 5+ minutes late to your original appointment.

If you have scheduled a meeting weeks in advance, please be open to have your meeting re-scheduled in accordance of my latest availability. I will notify of any updates over email.
