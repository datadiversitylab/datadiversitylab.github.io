We value your engagement and interest in the data diversity lab. Whether you have inquiries about our research, collaboration opportunities, or simply want to connect with our team, the contact us section is your gateway to open communication with us. We welcome your questions, feedback, and the opportunity to explore how our work aligns with your interests. Ways to reach us:

General inquiries Have a question or want to learn more about our research initiatives? Send us an email at cromanpa94@arizona.edu for general inquiries, and Dr. Román-Palacios get back to you promptly.
Collaboration opportunities Interested in exploring collaboration possibilities with the data diversity lab? Reach out to us at either cromanpa94@arizona.edu (Dr. Román-Palacios) or hdg1@arizona.edu (Hector Garcia), and let's discuss how we can work together.
Media and press Members of the media seeking information or interviews can contact our media relations team at cromanpa94@arizona.edu for prompt assistance.
Visit us If you're in the area and would like to visit the data diversity lab in person, please schedule your visit in advance by emailing Hector Garcia at hdg1@arizona.edu.

We appreciate your interest in the data diversity lab, and we look forward to hearing from you. Your inquiries and contributions play a vital role in shaping the collaborative and dynamic spirit of our community.
